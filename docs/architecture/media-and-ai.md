# Media and AI Review

Photos and videos are core ApiaryLens data.

## Media Types

- Brood frame photos
- Queen photos
- Queen cell photos
- Mite board photos
- Hive entrance photos
- Pest/disease photos
- Honey frame photos
- Harvest photos
- Weather damage photos
- Inspection videos

## AI Principles

AI is optional. AI must not be required for core functionality.

AI may flag observations, but must not provide definitive disease diagnosis.

Provider abstraction should allow:

- OpenAI
- Anthropic
- Local model
- Disabled mode
