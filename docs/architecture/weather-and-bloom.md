# Weather and Bloom Intelligence

ApiaryLens should connect hive behavior to local environment.

## Weather History

Track historical:

- Temperature high/low
- Rainfall
- Humidity
- Wind
- Frost dates
- Heat waves
- Cold snaps
- Storms
- Drought

## Bee Relevance

Correlate weather with:

- Inspection timing
- Honey flow
- Bloom shifts
- Winter feed consumption
- Population trends

## Bloom Calendar

Track plants by location, bloom window, nectar value, pollen value, and local observations.
