# ApiaryLens 0.1.0 Preview 1

## Release status

ApiaryLens Preview 1 is a **Public Preview**, not GA or a stable release. It is
intended for families, hobbyists, mentors, and operators who want to evaluate the
real product and help shape the next release. Features, workflows, and interfaces
may change without preserving every preview detail. Updates may arrive frequently,
including multiple times in a day.

Preview 1 carries forward the verified `0.1.0-rc.7` implementation and public
artifacts while the remaining owner and device acceptance work continues. See the
[MVP release gates](../testing/mvp-release-gates.md) and [MVP UAT record](../testing/mvp-uat.md)
for the evidence boundary.

## What you can evaluate

- A responsive, installable offline-capable PWA for apiaries, hives, queens,
  equipment, inspections, photos, health and mite records, feeding, treatments,
  harvests, and follow-ups.
- Family access with owner, beekeeper, and viewer roles, invitations, private media,
  synchronization, exports, backup, restore, and conflict recovery.
- A Cloudflare family profile and portable Docker Compose deployment, with Scout Bee
  guidance for installation, updates, diagnostics, recovery, and uninstall.
- Public project, user, operator, developer, security, and deployment documentation.

The synthetic public demo is safe to reset and must not receive real hive or identity
data. Use a private deployment for family records.

## Preview safety

Keep a current backup and export before every update or migration. Do not treat
ApiaryLens Preview 1 as the sole copy of irreplaceable hive records or media. Follow
the [backup and restore guide](../deployment/migration-guide.md), keep the release
artifact identity with your backup, and use the documented recovery path if an
update is interrupted.

Preview 1 is not a promise of permanent free provider allowances, uninterrupted
availability, or backward compatibility across every preview update. Review the
known limitations and release gates before using it for important work.

## Feedback and support

Report reproducible product issues with the release, deployment profile, browser or
device, and sanitized diagnostics where possible. Do not include passwords, tokens,
private hive locations, or irreplaceable media in an issue. Security and data-loss
risks should follow [SECURITY.md](../../SECURITY.md).

The [changelog](changelog.md), [roadmap](../roadmap/roadmap.md), and
[verification instructions](verification.md) are updated as Preview 1 evolves.
