# OpenHive
## Open Source Apiary Management Platform

### Vision
OpenHive is an open-source, self-hosted platform for managing everything from a single backyard hive to large commercial apiaries.

### MVP Highlights
- Multi-apiary management
- Hive, queen, box, and frame tracking
- Offline-first PWA
- Inspection logging
- Health and Varroa tracking
- Weather history
- Bloom calendar
- Photo & video history
- Honey harvest tracking
- Docker deployment
- REST API

### Future Roadmap
- AI photo analysis
- Mentor & club collaboration
- Sensor integration
- Home Assistant integration
- Native iOS & Android apps
- Research mode
- Plugin marketplace

### Guiding Principles
Open Source • Privacy First • Offline First • Self Hosted • AI Assisted • Community Driven
