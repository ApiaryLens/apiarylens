# OpenHive Project Overview (Living Document)

OpenHive is an open-source beekeeping application that helps individuals, families, clubs, and commercial beekeepers manage their apiaries.

Goals:
- Simple enough for first-time beekeepers.
- Powerful enough for large apiaries.
- Runs on your own computer or server.
- Works offline in the bee yard.
- Supports phones, tablets, and computers.

Highlights:
- Track unlimited apiaries and hives
- Record inspections
- Track queens, boxes, and frames
- Health and mite tracking
- Honey harvest records
- Weather and bloom calendars
- Photos and videos
- AI-assisted photo review
- Secure sharing with mentors and clubs
