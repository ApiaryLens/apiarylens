ApiaryLens
Pre-release project

Know every hive. Empower every beekeeper.

A privacy-first, offline-first hive record designed for families and hobbyist beekeepers—and built to grow with them.

One clear history for every hive

Offline inspections

Record notes and follow-up work in the bee yard, even without a signal.

Hive & queen history

Keep each colony, queen, apiary, and equipment timeline together.

Photos & videos

Build a searchable visual history connected to the right hive.

Health & treatments

Track Varroa results, observations, treatments, and follow-up checks.

Weather & blooms

Add useful environmental context to inspections and seasonal trends.

Share selectively

Work with family, mentors, or clubs without exposing everything.
